About Curlvation

Technology Built by Curlers, for Curlers

Founded in Grand Rapids, Minnesota, Curlvation was born from a passion for the sport and a belief that technology could make it better. We combine deep curling knowledge with modern engineering to create tools that solve real problems on the ice — whether you're timing a delivery, reading ice conditions across the sheet, or measuring stone placement with precision.

Our products are designed to be practical, durable, and intuitive — because the ice doesn't wait and neither should your tools.

Why Curlvation

Technology That Understands the Sport

We don't build generic sports tech. Every Curlvation product is engineered around the specific demands of curling — the physics of the stone, the chemistry of the ice, and the split-second decisions that determine the outcome of every end.

  • 01

    Built From the Ice Up

    Our products are designed by people who understand curling — not adapted from general sports tech. Every feature solves a real problem that curlers actually face.

  • 02

    Actionable Insights, Not Just Data

    RockTimer doesn't just show you a number — it tells you what that number means for sweeping decisions. IceReader doesn't just log conditions — it alerts you when action is needed.

  • 03

    Club & Competitive Ready

    Whether you're running a weekly league or preparing for a Bonspiel, Curlvation's tools scale from recreational to competitive play without compromise.

  • 04

    Rooted in Minnesota Curling Culture

    Based in Grand Rapids, MN — a community that takes its curling seriously — Curlvation is built with Midwest values: practical, dependable, and no-nonsense.
